Overview

Imagine planning surveillance camera placements in a space and wanting to evaluate coverage before any physical installation. Instead of costly real-world trial-and-error, you and a collaborator can wear AR headsets, place virtual cameras together, draw pedestrian routes on a map, and observe live camera streams — all in a shared AR scene.
