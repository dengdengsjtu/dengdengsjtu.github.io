High-efficiency Folded Optics for Near-eye Displays

IMAGE PLACEHOLDER

Overview

Pancake lens structures offer a promising path to compact near-eye displays, but the half-mirror that triples the optical path length limits theoretical efficiency to 25%. To reduce power consumption and battery weight, we designed a folded optical structure using two polarization-selective cholesteric liquid-crystal reflectors, achieving 50% optical efficiency with a doubled optical path length.
